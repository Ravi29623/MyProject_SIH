  • Help Center ▾
    • How can I book a counseling session?
      • You can book an appointment via the 'Book Appointment'. Fill out the form with your details and select a counselor.
    • Is my data private and secure?
      • Yes. All your information is encrypted and kept confidential. Only authorized counselors can access your session data.
    • Can I track my mood over time?
      • Yes. Use the Mood Tracking feature to view your mood trends and patterns over days or weeks.
    • What if I need immediate help?
      • You can use the Chatbot or check the Emergency Quick Help Demo in the 'Demo' section for immediate guidance.

Problem & Our Approach

Students face stress, anxiety, and academic pressure with limited access to timely support. Our solution combines AI triage, confidential counseling, and evidence-based wellness activities to deliver help where and when it's needed.

  • On-demand human counseling when needed
  • Privacy-first data handling
  • Personalized user dashboard and insights
  • Data analytics and reporting for institutional use
  • Integrated with academic and campus life services
